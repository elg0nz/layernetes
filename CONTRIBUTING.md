# Contributing to Layernetes

Layernetes is developed by [Sanscourier.ai](https://sanscourier.ai) and
licensed to [Learning Layer](https://www.learninglayer.ai/). The source is
published under [AGPL-3.0-or-later](LICENSE) so you can study, run, and fork
it — but **development is not open to unsolicited contributions**.

## Contribution is by application only

We do not accept unsolicited pull requests; they will be closed without
review. If you want to contribute to Layernetes, apply first:

**Email <business@sanscourier.ai>** with:

- Who you are and your background
- What you want to work on and why
- Links to relevant prior work (GitHub, papers, projects)

Accepted contributors will be asked to sign a contributor agreement granting
Sanscourier.ai the rights it needs to keep licensing Layernetes both under
the AGPL and commercially. Only after that agreement is in place will pull
requests be reviewed.

## What you can do without applying

- **Use it.** The AGPL grants you that; no permission needed.
- **Fork it.** Also your right under the AGPL — but note the license's
  obligations, including offering source to network users of modified
  versions.
- **Report bugs.** Issues with clear reproduction steps are welcome.
- **Report vulnerabilities.** Privately, per [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) —
  never as a public issue or PR.
